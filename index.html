1. Introduction

Welcome to Dil Ki Baat ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Information

We collect a unique device identifier to:

  • Enable device-based security features
  • Allow only the device that uploaded a story to delete it
  • Prevent unauthorized access to your content
  • Track device-specific usage patterns

2.2 Audio Content

When you upload audio stories, we collect:

  • Audio file content
  • Story title and metadata
  • Upload timestamp
  • Duration of audio

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Number of times stories are played (views count)
  • Search queries and browsing history
  • Playlist creation and modifications
  • App interaction patterns
  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash reports

2.4 Information You Provide

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Story titles and descriptions
  • Playlist names and descriptions
  • Content reports and feedback
  • Support requests and inquiries

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Uses

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • Content Management: To store, organize, and display your audio stories
  • Security: To implement device-based security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Analytics: To understand user behavior and improve user experience
  • Content Moderation: To monitor and enforce our content policy

3.2 Communication

We may use your information to:

  • Send service-related announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Notify you of policy violations or content removal
  • Provide updates about new features

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect against fraud or security threats
  • Protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of users

4. Data Security

🔒 Security Measures:

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted over HTTPS with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Secure Storage: Audio files stored on secure cloud servers (Appwrite)
  • Access Control: Role-based access control and authentication
  • Device Security: Device ID-based authorization for content deletion
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates
⚠️ Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your uploaded stories at any time
  • Delete your stories (if uploaded from your device)
  • Create and manage playlists
  • Control your device-based security settings

5.2 Data Deletion

You can delete your stories by:

  • Using the delete button on your story (only available on the device that uploaded it)
  • Contacting us for account-level deletion requests

5.3 Privacy Requests

You may request:

  • A copy of your personal data

  • Clarification about how your data is used
